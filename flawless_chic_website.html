About Us

Flawless Chic PTY (Ltd) was established in October 2021 by founder Refiloe Hlahane. The inspiration behind starting the brand came from a personal experience she encountered as a young woman going through the menstrual cycle for the first time. Our mission is to reinvent the design and display of sanitary pads to provide a calming, soothing, and pleasant experience for all women.

Mission: Producing the most comfortable, secure, and hygienic sanitary pads that will be vastly available at an affordable price, across all borders and more especially to the disadvantaged.

Vision: To provide comfort, relief, and a sense of security and awareness to women who go through menstruation, especially young girls who are new to the experience and changes to their body. To promote, expand, and normalize feminine hygiene across all borders.

Services & Products

We offer ultra-thin pads with wings that provide fast absorption, are unscented, and individually wrapped for hygienic purposes. Our products provide ultimate comfort throughout usage.
